What is a Wiki?

A wiki, most would say they are websites with editable information. The most used wiki (I think) is wikipedia.org. A wiki is much more than most would think, according to cft.vanderbilt.edu, a wiki is, "a collaborative tool that allows students to contribute and modify one or more pages of course related materials."
